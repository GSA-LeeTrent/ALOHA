The details below are the discrepancies that need to be reconciled for a single pay period. You must address all discrepancies for this pay period or none of your changes for the pay period will be submitted for correction. All changes are subject to manager approval before the leave discrepancy is resolved.
  • Under the Which Is Correct? column, choose ALOHA, ETAMS or Neither to indicate which system (if any) has the correct information recorded.
  • If you choose ALOHA:
    • The ALOHA Hours for that row will be outlined in green.
    • Once you review and confirm your changes, an ETAMS amendment will be created.
  • If you choose ETAMS:
    • The ETAMS Hours for that row will be outlined in green.
    • Once you review and confirm your changes, either a new ALOHA request will be created or an existing ALOHA request will be amended or withdrawn.
  • If you choose Neither:
    • Yellow boxes will appear in that row under ALOHA Hours and ETAMS Hours.
    • Enter the correct time in both yellow boxes. The times entered must be the same.
    • If no leave was taken, enter 0 in both boxes.
    • Once you review and confirm your changes, an ETAMS amendment and an ALOHA request will be generated.
